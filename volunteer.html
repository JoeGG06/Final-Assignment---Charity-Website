How to Volunteer

1. Apply Online: Fill out the volunteer application form with your details and availability.

2. Wait for Response: If selected, we will contact you for an interview.

3. Attend Orientation: Complete any required training to get ready for your role.

4. Start Volunteering: Once trained, you can begin your volunteer work!

Volunteer Opportunities

Feline Care Volunteers

Assist with cleaning and maintaining animal housing, and provide compassionate care through positive reinforcement training and socialization.

Team Experience Day Volunteers

Support corporate groups by facilitating building tours, animal interactions, and volunteer activities during scheduled events.

Canine Care Volunteers

Help ensure that canine housing is clean and comfortable, and engage in positive reinforcement training and socialization activities.

Pet Parent Support Network Volunteers

Provide assistance remotely by handling phone inquiries, responding to emails, and managing reports.

Special Species Care Volunteers

Care for various animal species, maintaining their environments, and implementing appropriate training and socialization techniques.

Adoption Support Volunteers

Help with organizing and assisting at adoption events, answer questions from potential adopters, and provide general support to ensure smooth operations.

Volunteer Requirements

• You must be over 18-years old

• All volunteer roles, unless otherwise specified during the onboarding process, require commitment to a weekly shift for a minimum of 6 months

• All interested candidates must fill out an application. Only successful applicants will be contacted for an interview

• We provide theoretical and hands-on training to all volunteers. Multiple-choice testing and AODA Customer Service Standard training are included in the training

• As part of our health and safety protocol, we strongly recommend that all volunteers have a current tetanus shot
