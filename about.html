About Toronto Humane Society

Founded in 1887, the Toronto Humane Society is dedicated to improving the lives of animals and fostering a compassionate society. We work to ensure that every animal is treated with respect and given the opportunity to thrive in a safe and caring environment.

Our mission is to promote humane care, prevent cruelty, and provide shelter, medical treatment, and education. We are committed to creating a world where all animals are valued, and we continue to advocate for their well-being through outreach, support, and innovative programs.

Why Choose Us

1. Experienced and Trusted: Over 130 years of dedication to animal welfare.

2. Comprehensive Care: We provide shelter, medical treatment, and behavioral support for animals in need.

3. Community-Focused: Strong programs that help both animals and their human companions.

4. Innovative Solutions: Leading the way with new approaches to animal care and welfare.

5. Compassionate Team: Our staff and volunteers are passionate about making a difference in the lives of animals.

Learn More About Our Community Services And Programs

The Toronto Humane Society offers adoption, veterinary care, pet support, and education. We also provide specialized services for senior pets and emergency rescue. Join us in supporting a compassionate community for animals and people! Join us in building a compassionate community where both pets and people thrive!
